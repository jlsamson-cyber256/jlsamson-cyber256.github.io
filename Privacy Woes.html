// privacy myth

Privacy is only for those with something to hide? Yes, very correct! As you kindly watch your data flow collectivistically—country to country, APT to APT, data broker to databroker to databroker

I am using a VPN? Uh huh? That FreeVerySafeVPN Ultra 360 Protection on Google Play Store?
I am using a Paid VPN? Uh huh? That superiorly over-marketed VPN that spends millions on advertisements and is almost recommended by everyone?

Choose a correct VPN—the heavier the marketing, the lower the protection.

// privacy vs protection

You can be law compliant without being data ignorant.

Data Privacy

Privacy is held by the people. The people have the right to their data.

Data Protection

Protection is held by the vendor. The vendor has the responsibility to protect its users' data, provide proper disclosures and transparency. This separation differentiates Privacy Marketers from Privacy Practitioners.

Privacy is a human right, not a luxury.
